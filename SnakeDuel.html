About Snake Duel

Objective: Control the green snake to eat food (blue) and health pickups (yellow) while avoiding collisions with yourself or the red enemy snake.

Controls: Use arrow keys to move the green snake (up, down, left, right).

Mechanics: Eating food grows your snake. Health pickups restore 1 life (max 5). If snakes collide, the shorter snake loses 1 life; if equal, both lose 1 life.

Maps: Normal: Snakes die on wall collisions. Toroidal: Snakes wrap around to the opposite side when hitting a wall.

Win/Lose: Win by reducing the enemy's life to 0 or if the enemy hits a wall or itself (Normal map). Lose if your snake hits a wall (Normal map), itself, or runs out of life.

Difficulty: Adjust speed with the difficulty selector (Easy: slower, Hard: faster).

Tip: Grow longer than the enemy to gain an advantage in collisions!
